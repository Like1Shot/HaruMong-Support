# Support & FAQ

Need help with HaruMong? Find answers to common questions below or contact us directly.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

#### What is HaruMong?
HaruMong is a habit tracking app that combines daily habit management with a virtual pet experience. Complete your habits to keep your pet happy and healthy!

#### Is HaruMong free?
Yes! HaruMong offers core features for free, including habit tracking, pet care, check-ins, and mood tracking. Premium features are available through subscription or a one-time lifetime purchase.

#### What languages does HaruMong support?
HaruMong currently supports:
- English
- Korean (한국어)

---

### Habits

#### How do I create a new habit?
1. Go to the **Habits** tab
2. Tap the **+** button
3. Choose a habit type (Simple or Quantity)
4. Enter the habit details
5. Tap **Save**

#### What's the difference between Simple and Quantity habits?
- **Simple habits**: Check off when completed (e.g., "Read a book", "Meditate")
- **Quantity habits**: Track measurable amounts (e.g., "Drink 2000mL water", "Walk 10000 steps")

#### How do I edit or delete a habit?
Long-press on any habit to see options for editing or deleting.

#### Why did my habit streak reset?
Streaks reset when you miss a day without completing or skipping the habit. Make sure to complete your habits daily to maintain your streak!

#### Can I skip a habit for a day?
Yes! Swipe left on a habit to skip it. Skipping won't break your streak but won't count as completion either.

### Pet

#### How do I keep my pet happy?
Your pet's happiness depends on:
- Completing daily habits
- Doing morning and evening check-ins
- Maintaining consistent streaks

#### What happens if I neglect my pet?
If you skip habits or miss check-ins, your pet's happiness and health will decrease. Don't worry—you can always improve by getting back on track!

#### Can I change my pet's name or color?
Yes! Go to **Settings** > **Pet Settings** to change your pet's name, color, or personality.

---

### Check-ins

#### What are check-ins?
Check-ins are brief daily reflections:
- **Morning check-in**: Start your day with intention
- **Evening check-in**: Reflect on your accomplishments

#### Do I have to do check-ins?
Check-ins are optional but recommended. They help maintain your pet's health and provide valuable reflection time.

### Data & Privacy

#### Where is my data stored?
Your data is stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud sync, it's also backed up to your personal iCloud account.

#### Will I lose my data if I delete the app?
If iCloud sync is enabled, your data will be restored when you reinstall. Without iCloud sync, deleting the app removes all data.

#### How do I export my data?
Currently, data export is not available. We're considering this feature for future updates.

#### How do I delete all my data?
Go to **Settings** > scroll to bottom > **Delete All Data**. This action cannot be undone.

### Premium & Subscriptions

#### What's included in Premium?
Premium includes:
- Additional themes and customization
- Premium widgets
- Ad-free experience
- All future premium features

#### What are my subscription options?
- **Monthly**: Billed monthly, cancel anytime
- **Yearly**: Billed annually, best value for subscribers
- **Lifetime**: One-time purchase, permanent access

#### How do I restore my purchase?
1. Go to **Settings**
2. Scroll to **Premium**
3. Tap **Restore Purchases**

#### How do I cancel my subscription?
1. Open **Settings** on your iPhone
2. Tap your **Apple ID** at the top
3. Tap **Subscriptions**
4. Find **HaruMong** and tap **Cancel Subscription**

#### I purchased Premium but still see ads
Try these steps:
1. Restart the app completely
2. Go to Settings > Premium > Restore Purchases
3. If the issue persists, contact us

### Widgets

#### How do I add widgets to my home screen?
1. Long-press on your home screen
2. Tap the **+** button (top left)
3. Search for "HaruMong"
4. Choose a widget size and tap **Add Widget**

#### Why isn't my widget updating?
Widgets update periodically to save battery. Try:
1. Opening the app to trigger an update
2. Removing and re-adding the widget
3. Restarting your device

---

### Technical Issues

#### The app is crashing
Try these steps:
1. Force quit and restart the app
2. Restart your device
3. Update to the latest version
4. If issues persist, contact us

#### My data isn't syncing
1. Check that iCloud is enabled in Settings
2. Ensure you have enough iCloud storage
3. Check your internet connection
4. Try signing out and back into iCloud

#### HealthKit data isn't showing
1. Go to **Settings** > **Privacy & Security** > **Health**
2. Find **HaruMong** and enable the required permissions
3. Restart the app
